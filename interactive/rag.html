RAG vs Fine-Tuning

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

✅ No retraining needed
✅ Always up to date (add new docs anytime)
✅ Cites sources with references
✅ Cheap to set up and maintain
✅ Knowledge is transparent and auditable
❌ Slower (retrieval adds latency)
❌ Limited by retrieval quality

Fine-Tuning

✅ Knowledge baked into the model
✅ Fast inference (no retrieval step)
✅ Can learn new styles/behaviors
❌ Requires retraining for updates
❌ Can become outdated quickly
❌ No source citations
❌ Expensive GPU compute needed
