Real-World Numbers

GPT-3 (175B parameters):

Standard training: ~350 GB activations — impossible on any single GPU!

With checkpointing: ~35 GB activations — fits on 4x A100 (80GB each)

Memory reduction: 10x at the cost of ~33% more computation time

Analogy: Climbing a Building

Standard: Taking a photo at every floor as you go up. When you need to go back down, you have all the photos — but your camera roll is huge!

Checkpointing: Taking photos only every 6 floors. When you need a photo from floor 8, you walk back up from floor 6 and retake it. Your camera roll stays small, but you walk a bit more.
