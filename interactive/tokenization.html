Fun comparisons:
• In English, 1 token ≈ 4 characters on average.
• In Japanese, 1 token ≈ 1–2 characters (kanji are rarer in vocabulary).
• In Spanish, 1 token ≈ 3.5 characters.
• Code tends to be ~2.5 chars/token because of short variable names and symbols.
• GPT-4's vocabulary has ~100,000 tokens. Common words like "the" are single tokens, while rare words get split into subwords.
