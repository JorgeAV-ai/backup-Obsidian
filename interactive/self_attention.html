The Query-Key-Value Analogy

Q = "What am I looking for?"  |  K = "What do I offer?"  |  V = "What's my actual content?"

Each word creates a Query, Key, and Value. The Query asks a question, Keys from other words answer how relevant they are, and Values provide the actual information that gets mixed in.
